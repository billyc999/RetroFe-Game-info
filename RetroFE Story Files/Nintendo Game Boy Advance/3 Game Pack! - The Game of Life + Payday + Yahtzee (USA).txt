Yahtzee / Payday / Life is a compilation of great board games that many of us played when we were young. Every now and then, you might still break it out for old times' sake. Well, now it's easier than ever -- they've been shrunk down and fitted into your Game Boy Advance. See if you can become a millionaire in the Game Of Life, get the highest rolls in Yahtzee or see if you can earn big bucks in Payday! 
Breakout - SMASH! POW! CRUNCH! A brick wall appear on the screen and your mission is to knock it down, brick by brick. • Centipede - Watch out! Here comes a slithering Centipede, a poisonous scorpion, a mischievous spider and pesky flea. Aim your magic wand and shoot to stop these pests in their tracks. • Warlords - King Frederik's warlords have been battling for centuries. Now four young men are locked into the feud, with magic weapons like lightning bolts and fireballs.
