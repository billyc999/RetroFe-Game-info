Two games included on one cartridge.
Hang-On is a motorcycle racing game where the player must race against time and try to meet each checkpoint before the time runs out. Other racers are present and the player must pass and dodge them to hurry on to the next level.
Astro Warrior is a shoot-em-up game where the player must travel through space and defeat the enemies that come to the player, including the bosses at the end of each level. The player starts out with a single shot weapon that can be upgraded by collecting power-ups that will improve his or her weapon. The player must battle on through the levels.
